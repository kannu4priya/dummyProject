Tamanna Saini - Mealtime Delhi



She was adopted at 18 months by a French couple from Versailles. She spent many summers in Brittany at the family farm. Her mother, a cook with an "adventurous palate, took her young daughter to experience Indian, Chinese, Japanese, and Vietnamese restaurants in Paris.
Then she came back to India when she became aware of the fact that her parents belonged to India and her roots are here in this country.
